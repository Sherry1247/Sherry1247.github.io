About Me

I am a first-year student at UW-Madison double majoring in Computer Science and Economics, with a Mathematics Certificate. I am driven by the potential of data science and machine learning to solve complex physical and socioeconomic challenges.

Currently, I am participating in the Undergraduate Research Scholarship (URS) program under the guidance of Dr. Saurabh Gupta. My research focuses on multi-parameter virtual sensing for internal combustion engines using artificial neural networks.

In the upcoming summer, I am excited to join the AR/VR research project led by Prof. Yuhang Zhao, where I will be working under the mentorship of Yuheng Wu to explore intelligent interactive systems.
